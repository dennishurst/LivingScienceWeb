08/30/2013 Technology Association of Georgia Recognizes Award Finalists focused on Science, Technology, Engineering and Math Education /site/img/LS/TAG.png Lance Davis ATLANTA (August 30, 2013) – The Technology Association of Georgia (TAG), the state’s leading association dedicated to the promotion and economic advancement of Georgia’s technology industry, today announced that Living Science has been named as a Finalist in the Middle School category for the 2013 Georgia STEM Education Awards.
